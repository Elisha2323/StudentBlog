Places that are worth visiting.

la crosse, wisconsin

Lake Onalaska. La Crosse County

Lake Onalaska, located in La Crosse County, Wisconsin, is a serene freshwater body known for its natural beauty and recreational opportunities. Situated along the Mississippi River, it offers visitors picturesque views and a tranquil atmosphere perfect for fishing, boating, and birdwatching. The lake is surrounded by lush greenery and diverse wildlife, making it a popular destination for nature enthusiasts and outdoor adventurers alike. Whether you're looking to unwind by the water's edge or explore its scenic surroundings, Lake Onalaska provides a peaceful retreat in the heart of Wisconsin's stunning landscape.



osaka, japan

During the Sakura season

Greetings from Osaka during sakura season, where the city is adorned in delicate shades of pink and white! Today, I wandered through parks and gardens bursting with cherry blossoms, their petals dancing in the spring breeze. Along the Okawa River, hanami parties filled the air with laughter and joy as friends and families gathered under the blooming trees. The iconic Osaka Castle grounds transformed into a sea of sakura, creating a picturesque backdrop against the ancient fortress walls. This season truly encapsulates the beauty and fleeting nature of cherry blossoms, a time when Osaka radiates with enchanting springtime charm.



las pinias, metro manila

Vista mall

Greetings from Vista Mall Philippines, where shopping meets leisure in a vibrant atmosphere! Today, I explored the expansive halls filled with a variety of shops offering everything from fashion to electronics. The bustling food court delighted my taste buds with a diverse array of local and international cuisines. As I wandered through the mall, I found cozy corners to relax and enjoy the lively ambiance. Vista Mall embodies a perfect blend of convenience and entertainment, making it a favorite destination for families and friends alike to unwind and indulge in shopping and dining pleasures.

Timeless Dreams

A "Timeless Dreams," Jenna Rink wakes up as a 30-year-old woman, having wished away her teenage years. Now grappling with adulthood in a world she doesn't recognize, she embarks on a journey to reconnect with her inner child and rediscover the dreams she left behind. Through humor, heart, and a touch of magic, Jenna learns that growing up isn't about the years passed but the joy found in embracing who you truly are.

Spirited away
Whispers from the spirit realm

Chihiro finds herself trapped in a mysterious and enchanting spirit world after her parents undergo a bizarre transformation. As she navigates this strange realm, working in a bathhouse for spirits and encountering fantastical beings, Chihiro learns valuable lessons about courage, compassion, and the resilience of the human spirit. Through her journey, she discovers her own strength and the power of empathy, ultimately finding a way to save her family and herself from the mystical forces at play.
